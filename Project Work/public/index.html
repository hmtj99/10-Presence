Presence digitizes the way in which we take and track attendance

  • Email Attendance
  • Attendance Metrics

Presence uses a email based system to take attendance of students in the lectures

Time Constrained Links

The links sent to students are valid only for a certain amount of time and hence can makes sure that students can't mark their attendance at any random time and need to stay attentive.

Unique Links

Every link sent is unique to the student and the lecture ensuring that the link cannot be sent to other students and be misused.

Presence helps you track attendance metrics for both Professors as well as Students

Attendance Dashboard

Instructors can view the attendance percentage of every student and a birds eye view of the attendance for the course. Students can track their attendance in every course with all the relevant data.

Export Data

Instructors can download student attendance data in csv format for use in other applications.
